AI Core

Why InsightFace buffalo_s?

The Engine
  • 512-dim cosine similarityvs 128-dim euclidean (dlib) — higher discriminability
  • ONNX CPU inferenceNo GPU needed — runs on weak VPS at ~4ms detect
  • Two-phase pipelineMediaPipe (120ms, client) → InsightFace (9ms, server)
  • Multi-face in one passencode_all() — entire frame, all faces simultaneously
